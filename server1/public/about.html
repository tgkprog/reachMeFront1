About ReachMe

ReachMe is a critical alert system designed to ensure you never miss important notifications from your loved ones or critical systems.

How it Works

ReachMe allows authorized users to send high-priority alerts to your device. These alerts are designed to grab your attention even when your device might be in a state where you'd normally miss notifications.

Key Features

  • Critical Alerts: Receive loud, distinct notifications for urgent matters.
  • Secure Access: Only authorized users you explicitly approve can send you alerts.
  • History: Keep a log of all received alerts for future reference.
  • Cross-Platform: Works on web and mobile devices.

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. Your data is encrypted, and we never share your personal information with third parties. You have full control over who can reach you.
