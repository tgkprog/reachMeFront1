About

ReachMe is a critical notification system designed to ensure important alerts reach you even when your device is on Do Not Disturb mode. Perfect for on-call professionals, emergency contacts, and anyone who needs guaranteed notification delivery.

How It Works

  1. Login with your authorized account (Google/GitHub/Facebook)
  2. Grant all required permissions when prompted
  3. The app polls your server at regular intervals for new commands
  4. When an alert is received, it plays an alarm (even in DND mode) and shows an overlay
  5. Use Sleep mode to temporarily stop checking for alerts
  6. Use Mute mode to check for alerts but not play alarms

Privacy & Security

  • All sensitive data is stored in encrypted storage on device
  • OAuth tokens are never logged or transmitted insecurely
  • Downloaded audio files are stored in app-private storage
  • No data is collected or shared with third parties
